The Bank of Saito attempts to keep your tokens active by periodically resending them across the blockchain. The fees necessary to transmit payments will be deducted from your account.

To use the Bank of Saito, select the bank module from within our default email client. You will have the option of depositing money, checking your balance, or withdrawing tokens.

Please be aware that this software is under-development, and the developers make no guarantees of security or token permanence. A small amount of tokens will also be removed from your account over time as part of the process of keeping your tokens on-chain. If you are concerned about protecting your SAITO tokens, and want full control over the process you should use our regular tools to rebroadcast your transactions manually.
